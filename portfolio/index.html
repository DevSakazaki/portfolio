Hello, I like a somewhat controversial phrase that says "The ends justify the means" (in a positive sense!!). In my case, this phrase makes sense because if someone is reading this today, it's because of the means I decided to follow until now. I have been studying technology and programming since I was 15 years old, and it's a truly captivating field, especially Data Science, which I find fascinating. Every time I learn something new, I fall more and more in love with it. I really like the concept of the T-shaped professional, who is a generalist in something but specializes in one area or sub-area of that profession, and that's exactly my case. I have knowledge in Data Science and I have delved into Natural Language Processing! I also have knowledge in computer science in general, as well as web and mobile programming.
What attracts me the most about Data Science is that it is completely futuristic. There are always advancements and something new, especially in the field of Machine Learning and Deep Learning, which has been evolving since the 1940s! My main goal as a Data Scientist is to help people with this technology and make their lives easier.
Putting Data Science aside for a moment, another area that I am passionate about is photography (which I promote on my Instagram). I love walking the streets of São Paulo and showing the details and beauty that few people can see in the rush of this city...
